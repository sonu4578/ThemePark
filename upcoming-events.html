Upcoming Events

Holiday Light Spectacular

Explore the park illuminated by millions of sparkling lights, forming mesmerizing displays, tunnels, and interactive exhibits. Highlights include a giant Christmas tree lighting ceremony and a breathtaking fireworks finale.

Ice Kingdom

Challenge yourself at the Ice Kingdom, featuring an ice-skating rink, snowball arenas. Families can enjoy icy-themed games, winter snacks, and live performances by frosty characters.

Santa’s Workshop

Step into Santa’s magical workshop, where kids can craft holiday ornaments, write letters to Santa, and meet him for a photo! Enjoy live performances by cheerful elves, storytelling sessions, and a glimpse of Santa’s reindeer.

Picture with Santa

Capture the magic of the season with a special visit to Santa! Families and children can take memorable photos with Santa Claus in his beautifully decorated winter wonderland.
